Reporting System

A web based application

1. To monitor and track children, who are admitted with severe acute malnutrition in Malnutrition Treatment Centres (MTCs).

2. For providing real time information on progress of severely acute malnourished children.

3. For planning, evaluation and use of information for action in facility based management of severe acute malnutrition.

1. Tracking of child using unique ID.

2. Alerts for adverse outcomes.

3. Sends voice based SMS Reminder to parents/ care takers for follow-up visits.

4. Dashboard features for analysis and infomration use for evidence based action.
